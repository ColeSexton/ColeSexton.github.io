A Little About Me:

I am a musician, audio engineer, and software developer.
I have experience developing front-end applications using JavaScript, React, HTML, and CSS.
I also work with Java and MySQL for back-end development and database management.
I am skilled in Logic Pro X and Pro Tools for creating, mixing, and mastering music.
I’m a dedicated sound engineer with experience mixing all types of live events, including rock/pop concerts, orchestras, corporate speaking engagements, and any event requiring sound reinforcement.
I’m well-versed in industry-standard mixing consoles from Avid, Allen & Heath, Yamaha, and Midas.
My goal is to use my knowledge and passion to create innovative music software and bridge the gap between creators and their audiences.
I love to learn, and I value integrity and honesty above all.
